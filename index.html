How Unnamed Stray Came To Be!

I’ve always loved creating, and for the past two years, I’ve been designing and handmaking earrings inspired by color, whimsy, and storytelling. For three years, my family cared for a stray cat who quickly became part of our lives, even though everyone had a different name for him. After he passed, I decided to name my earring brand in his honor, as a small tribute to the quiet joy he brought us. Every piece I make carries that same spirit: playful, thoughtful, and made with love. Thank you so much for the support, it means the world to me!
